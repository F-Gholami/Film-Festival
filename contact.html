About The Organization


"Boston Outdoor Film Festival" just got the approval to show movies at Boston Public Garden this year from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight.

We can't wait to help the public discover these awesome films!


The Boston Outdoor Film Festival, is an International, competitive festival. This mission is to provide a public forum in Boston in order to advance public interest in films and the independent production of films. To draw worldwide attention to Boston as a center for cinema. To encourage the rights of all Boston residents to access and experience the power of independent filmmaking, and to promote artistic excellence and the creative freedom of artists without censure. Boston Outdoor Film Festival, inc. is a not-for-profit organization. Boston Outdoor Film Festivals is a summer must that has earned a large and loyal following. Since its launch in 2000, we’ve wowed, entertained, thrilled, and pulled at the heartstrings of over 500,000 moviegoers, making our program one of New York City’s favorite summer film series. This event takes place in July and August. There is no better place to be on a hot summer night than watching a classic film and enjoying the breeze off of the East River from Harbor View Lawn with the dazzling Manhattan skyline before you. "Boston Outdoor Film Festival" just got the approval to show movies at Boston Bridge Park this year from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight. Showtime starts every day from 6pm til midnight. Tickets are free!
